About This Hymn Management System

At Flexora, we believe worship should be organized, seamless, and deeply meaningful. Under the leadership of our CEO, Hossana Korosi Maidoki, we created this Hymn Management System to help churches efficiently plan and manage their hymns for every Sunday service.

Why This System?

Selecting hymns can sometimes feel repetitive, rushed, or unstructured. This system provides a well-organized way to manage hymn selection, ensuring variety while keeping records of past hymns for better planning.

How It Works

  • Automatically suggests hymns based on previous records and themes.
  • Prevents repetition by keeping track of hymn usage.
  • Gives worship leaders and choirs a structured plan for each service.
